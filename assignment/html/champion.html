Ezreal

Rising Spell Force

Ezreal generates a stack of Rising Spell Force for each enemy hit by his abilities, which lasts 6 seconds, refreshes on subsequent hits and stacks up to 5 times.
RISING SPELL FORCE: For each stack, Ezreal gains 10% bonus attack speed, up to a maximum of 50%.

Mystic Shot

ACTIVE: Ezreal fires a bolt of energy in the target direction that deals physical damage to the first enemy hit and applies on-hit and Pix, Faerie on-attack effects at 100% effectiveness.

Essence Flux

ACTIVE: Ezreal fires an orb in the target direction that marks the first enemy champion, epic monster, or structure hit for 4 seconds. His next basic attack or ability against the target detonates the mark, dealing them magic damage.

Arcane Shift

ACTIVE: After the cast time, Ezreal blinks from his current location up-to 475 units towards the target location, firing a homing bolt that deals magic damage to the nearest enemy and briefly reveals them.

Trueshot Barrage

ACTIVE: Ezreal fires an energy projectile in the target direction, which briefly grants sight of the area it flies through and deals magic damage to enemies hit.

Kha'Zix

Unseen Threat

INNATE: Kha'Zix gains Unseen Threat whenever the enemy loses sight of him or he activates Void Assault Void Assault. UNSEEN THREAT: Kha'Zix's next basic attack against an enemy champion deals 14 − 116 (based on level) (+ 40% bonus AD) bonus magic damage and slows them by 25% for 2 seconds.

Taste Their Fear

PASSIVE: Kha'Zix considers any enemy unit to be Isolated if they are not nearby to one of their allies. Taste Their Fear Taste Their Fear, Evolved Reaper Claws Evolved Reaper Claws, and Evolved Spike Racks Evolved Spike Racks have special interactions against Isolated targets.
ACTIVE: Kha'Zix slashes the target enemy, dealing physical damage, increased by 110% against Isolated targets.

Void Spike

ACTIVE: Kha'Zix fires a bolt of spikes in the target direction that explodes upon hitting an enemy, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies.

Leap

ACTIVE: Kha'Zix leaps to the target location, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies upon landing.

Void Assault

PASSIVE: Each rank in Void Assault allows Kha'Zix to evolve one of his abilities, granting it additional effects. Choosing an ability initiates a 2 second cast time, during which Kha'Zix advances his form, receiving the evolution once it completes.
ACTIVE: Kha'Zix becomes Twilight invisible for 1.25 seconds, during which he gains 40% bonus movement speed.

Bard

Traveler's Call

INNATE - ANCIENT CHIMES: Bard's presence causes sacred Traveler's Call chimes to appear at random locations and linger for up to 10 minutes. Collecting a chime grants Bard Movement speed 24% bonus movement speed out of combat for 7 seconds, stacking up to 5 times, with every other chime collected beyond the first granting an additional 14% bonus movement speed, up to a total of 80% bonus movement speed, 20 + (1 per minute after 5 minutes) experience, and 12% maximum mana. Bard empowers his Meeps each time he collects 5 chimes.

Cosmic Binding

ACTIVE: Bard fires an energy bolt in the target direction that deals magic damage to the first enemy hit and slows them by 60% for a duration.

Caretaker's Shrine

ACTIVE: Bard conjures a shrine at the target location that builds up in power over 10 seconds, granting sight of a small area around it for 1.8 seconds. Up to 3 shrines can be active at once, which last until they are destroyed by champions stepping on them.

Magical Journey

ACTIVE: Bard opens a one-way magical corridor through a piece of terrain in the target direction for 10 seconds. Magical Journey will not cast unless it can open a corridor.
A champion can pass through the corridor by selecting it, becoming revealed during the travel. Bard and allies travel through the portal at 33% increased speed.

Tempered Fate

ACTIVE: Bard sends magical energy arcing to the target location, granting sight of the area during travel. Upon impact, it puts all units within into stasis for 2.5 seconds, as well as stunning all enemy champions, minions, and turrets struck for the same duration. Enemies hit are revealed for the duration.

Ezreal

Guerrilla Warfare

INNATE: Teemo gains Twilight invisibility after 1.5 seconds without moving, taking damage, performing actions that break stealth, channeling, or being in stasis. Teemo will maintain stealth so long as he remains idle and is not displaced. While in brush, Teemo gains the stealth even while moving and can move without breaking stealth.

Blinding Dart

ACTIVE: Teemo shoots a dart at the target enemy that deals magic damage and blinds them for a few seconds.

Move Quick

PASSIVE: Teemo gains bonus movement speed after 5 seconds without taking damage from enemy champions or turrets.
ACTIVE: Teemo gains bonus movement speed for 3 seconds. This overrides the passive bonus, but prevents the passive effect from being removed.

Toxic Shot

PASSIVE: Teemo's basic attacks deal bonus magic damage on-hit and inflict poison.

Noxious Trap

ACTIVE: Teemo tosses a poisonous mushroom to the target location that becomes Noxious stealthed after arming over 1 second, lasting for up to 5 minutes and granting sight of its surroundings. The mushroom will bounce forward upon landing on another.

Pantheon

Mortal Will

INNATE: Pantheon generates a stack of Mortal Will whenever he lands a basic attack on-hit or casts an ability, stacking up to a maximum of 5. At 5 stacks, Pantheon's next basic ability consumes the stacks to become empowered with an additional effect.

Comet Spear

ACTIVE: Pantheon charges while being slowed by 10% for up to 4 seconds to increase Comet Spear's range after 0.35 seconds of channeling. Comet Spear can be recast within the duration.

Shield Vault

ACTIVE: Pantheon leaps to the front of the target enemy's location and, if the target is within 800 units upon arrival, he deals physical damage and stuns them for 1 second.

Aegis Assault

ACTIVE: Pantheon braces his shield in the target direction and channels for 1.5 seconds, during which he becomes Cosmic invulnerable against non-Turret turret damage dealt by enemies from the target direction. He also continually performs strikes in a cone in front of him, dealing 8.3% AD physical damage every 0.125 seconds to enemies hit, reduced by 50% against minions and up to 100% AD total physical damage against each target.

Grand Starfall

ACTIVE: Pantheon channels for 2 seconds, then leaps high into the air, vanishing and becoming immune to crowd control until he reappears. While in the air, Pantheon channels again for 2.2 seconds and grants sight around the target location 0.5 seconds into the channel.

Fiora

Duelist's Dance

INNATE: Fiora identifies the Vitals of nearby visible enemy champions, marked as an arc around them in North-, East-, South- or Westward direction. Vitals take 1.75 seconds to become targetable and linger for 13.25 seconds afterwards while Fiora remains near the target, otherwise the Vital will disappear. After a Vital ends, Fiora identifies a new one on her target.

Lunge

ACTIVE: Fiora dashes a short distance in the target direction and then stabs a nearby enemy. Lunge can hit structures and wards. Stabbing a target reduces Lunge's Cooldown cooldown by 50%.

Riposte

ACTIVE: Fiora enters a defensive stance for 0.75 seconds, becoming unable to act but preventing the application of all non-turret damage to her and gaining debuff immunity and crowd control immunity.

Edit Bladework

ACTIVE: Fiora's next two basic attacks Pix, Faerie on-attack within 4 seconds gain 25 bonus range and bonus attack speed.

Grand Challenge

ACTIVE: Fiora challenges the target enemy champion for 8 seconds, highlighting all four of their Vitals after a 0.5-second delay. While in effect, Duelist's Dance Duelist's Dance does not identify new Vitals on Fiora's target.
