Penang Hill

   Penang Hill, Pulau Penang
  

About

Penang Hill, also known as Penang Hill, is located in the central part of Penang Island. Its main peak, Western Hill, is 833 meters above sea level, which is the highest point in Penang. Among them, Qishan, which is 735 meters above sea level, is the most developed mountain among the peaks of Penang Mountain, and it is also a summer resort for local residents and tourists. Because most of the villas of high-ranking British officials in the past were here, the soldiers at the foot of the mountain used semaphore to convey important messages, hence the name Penang Hill. Penang Hill has a cool and pleasant climate and is a famous tourist destination in Penang.

  

What can we do?

Hop off the flag mountain train

The most convenient way to get to Penang Hill is to take the train to the top!
Located at the foot of Penang Hill, this Swiss-designed train runs through the world's oldest train system, taking you through lush green forests.

Lock love

This love lock area is very popular (especially couples), but single people or with a group of friends also like to come here, you can take a bunch of IG photos!

Visit some historic buildings

If you are a history buff, you can embark on this historical trail journey to see some of the famous buildings on Penang Hill.
